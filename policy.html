Policies

Everything you need to know about how we work — no surprises, just clarity. Questions? Reach out anytime.

Deposit & Payment

A 50% deposit is required at the time of booking to hold your date. The remaining balance is due one week before your event.

We accept payment through Square (credit/debit card) and Venmo.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. Here's how cancellations work:

  • 30 or more days before your event: Full refund, including your deposit.
  • Less than 30 days before your event: The deposit is non-refundable. Any balance paid beyond the deposit will be refunded in full.

What's Included

Every package includes 3 hours of booth time, setup and teardown (not counted toward your time), unlimited prints during your event, and an on-site attendant for the full duration.

Add-ons such as extra hours, custom overlays, backdrops, props, and guest books are available — see the Pricing page for details.

Travel

We are based in Utah and serve the surrounding area. Travel fees may apply for events outside our local range — contact us with your venue location and we'll let you know upfront.

Marketing & Photos

We may use photos from your event in our portfolio and on social media. If you'd prefer to opt out, just let us know in writing before your event and we'll respect that completely — no questions asked.

Liability

Eventful Memories LLC is not responsible for injuries, damages, or losses beyond our reasonable control. We take great care with our equipment and your event space.

Friends & Family Discounts

We offer discounts to close friends and family as a personal gesture — not a business obligation. Discount tiers are set by Katy and Lorenne and are non-negotiable. Asking for a tier you don't qualify for, or pressuring us to make exceptions, will result in the discount being withdrawn entirely.

Not sure if you qualify or what tier you're in? Use the Friends & Family quiz — it'll tell you exactly where you land. If you still have questions, reach out and we'll clarify, but the answer is final.

Discount tiers at a glance:

  • Immediate family & closest friends: First 2 events free, 1 event at cost, 25% off all events after that.
  • Extended family & close friends: 20% off.
  • Friends & family of Katy or Lorenne: 15% off.

Let's Work Together

Whether you're a photographer, venue, or event organizer — or just someone who wants a great photo booth — we'd love to connect. Referral partnerships, vendor collabs, and recurring event setups are always on the table.
